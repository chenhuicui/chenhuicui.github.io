About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST), advised by Prof. Rubing Huang. I received my M.S. degree (2022) and B.S. degree (2019) from Jiangsu University.

I work at the intersection of AI for Software Engineering and software testing, focusing on mobile/web GUI testing and diversity testing (e.g., Adaptive Random Testing (ART)).

I am open to research collaboration. If you are interested in mobile testing, GUI modeling, or AI4SE, feel free to reach out: 3230002105@student.must.edu.mo.

üöÄ Research Thrust

"I combine program analysis, testing data, and representation learning to infer GUI navigation models (e.g., Activity/Page Transition Graphs) that better capture feasible transitions and their triggering widgets. These models support downstream tasks such as automated exploration, test generation, and regression testing in real-world mobile and web applications."

GUI Modeling for Mobile/Web Testing

My research focuses on Android and web application testing, especially GUI testing, model-based testing, and automated test generation/exploration for real-world systems.

Testing Methodologies & AI4SE

I am also interested in principled testing methodologies, including Adaptive Random Testing (ART), and how AI techniques (e.g., LLMs) can assist software testing and quality assurance. My goal is to bridge practical testing workflows with data-driven methods to improve effectiveness under limited budgets.
